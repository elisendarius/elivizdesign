The approach

Science-first.
Design-driven.

Every visualization begins with understanding the science — the data structure, the hypothesis, the audience. Design choices follow scientific logic, not the other way around.

With a background in animal science and data analysis, I bridge the gap between complex quantitative results and clear visual communication. Whether it's a genome-wide association study, a breeding value distribution, or a multi-trait correlation matrix — if it has structure, it has a visual story.
